### Desktop (1280px+)
- 2-column score cards

- Centered content (max-w-7xl)

### Tablet (768px - 1279px)
- 2-column score cards
- Full-width form
- Adjusted padding

### Mobile (<768px)
- Single column layout
- Stacked score cards
- Compressed padding
- Smaller typography
